|I can see this score in the vibe platform, right above my video. What is that? |The score you see above your video on on the right side in ViBe is known as penalty score. ViBe is an AI-enabled learning platform which proctors your activity while watching the videos. It makes sure if your are paying attention while watching the videos. If there is someone besides you, the penalty score goes up. If someone is speaking, it goes up. If someone is moving their head, it goes up.|Please note: Penalty Score does not have any affect on your HP (Health Points) during the course of your internship. However, it is to alert you to watch the videos attentively and in favourable conditions. |
